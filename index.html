About Us

We were born with a simple mission: to make snack breaks a moment of well-being. We select only the best natural, organic, and low-environmental-impact products.

We believe vending shouldn't be synonymous with junk food, but an opportunity to recharge with clean energy.

Our Heartbeat:
Partnership with "Time to Help"

With every snack sold, you join us in giving back. We donate £0.05 from every purchase to our official charity partner, "Time to Help."

This incredible London-based association transforms these contributions into nutritious meals for the city’s homeless population. Choosing MindTheSnack means your team’s break time directly supports those in need.
